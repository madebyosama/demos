Before you book

Your questions, answered

Quick answers to what most people want to know. Anything else? Call us on +44 7886 304409.

Do I need a referral from my GP?
No. As a private clinic you can book directly with us — there's no need to wait for a GP referral, and you can usually be seen within the same week.
How soon can I be seen?
We keep appointments available for new patients and aim to see you within a few days. Call or request a callback and we'll find a time that works.
Can you treat me at home?
Yes. We offer home-visit physiotherapy across Birmingham — ideal after surgery or when travelling is difficult — as well as online video sessions.
What happens in the first session?
Your physiotherapist assesses the cause of your pain, explains what's going on, and begins hands-on treatment. You'll leave with a clear, personalised plan.
Where are you based?
Our clinic is at G04, Beech House, Greenfield Crescent, Edgbaston, Birmingham B15 3BE — a short walk from Five Ways.
Can I claim on private health insurance?
Many patients claim through private insurance and we can provide receipts for your provider. Get in touch and we'll confirm what your policy needs.
